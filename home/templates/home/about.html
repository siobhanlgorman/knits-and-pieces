About Knits and Pieces

I have been crafting since I was in primary school, taught by my two expert grannies to knit and crochet. I find working with my hands a source of comfort, relaxation and an opportunity to experiment with creativity.

I had been making hats and blankets just for myself and family until friends started to request for themselves. And so Knits and Pieces was born.


Over the last couple of years the sewing machine also came out as pretty and comfortable cotton facemasks were in demand. Who would have thought we'd still be wearing them?


I hope you enjoy my creations!


Siobhan
